AREAS TO VISIT

BASU CAFE

Basu Cafe situated just in front of our college is the oldest hangout place near our college. Having a wide array of chinese dishes it is one of the best vellapanti addas in our college's vicinity. Chilly Potato and Chowmien being the best are must try outs at Basu. It also has a open sitting place at it's backyard. Though small in terms of area Basu provides quality service and Food.

SADDA ADDA

Sadda Adda being far more spacious than Basu has a much better ambience .Food is just satisfactory.As an add-on it has better seating facilities and hookah too. Basic stationary items like practical files and photocopy facilities are also available at Sadda Adda. All in all it is a compelete fun-filled place though one will find loads of smoke due to persistent usage of hookah and cigarettes.

TAI

It would be unfair to call Tai an eatery it is just a small low budget stall owned by an aged woman. Having basic variety of chips it is a wise choice for satisfying small hunger. Tai's stall is located just in front of our college on left hand side. Keeping in mind our college's location even tai has it's own significance among collge students.

GURUKRIPA DHABA

Guru Kripa Dhaba is a proper full fledged eatery .It is located on backside of the college . It is first prefrence of all when it comes to "Desi Khaana". It is a boon for students living in PG near our college. Having roti sabzi daal on it's Menu it satisfies taste as well as hunger very nicely. It is a must try out for all college peeps.
