ABOUT US

Sadda College is a website/ web app for the students of HMRITM through which they get information they need to know and is not provided by the official website of college .It contains wide array of stuff like Faculty reviews; areas to visit ; unofficial societies ; detailed insight into ideal methodology for exam preparation and job hunting ; notes , important topics , faculty expert ,books pdf , tutorial links of each subject of each and every branch ; tags given to students ; gallery ; HMR Times containing all the official and unofficial events going on in college ; integrated messaging service to interact with students throughout the college ; built in camera service to click selfies ; selfie contest is held on daily basis ; gaming zone.
So, all in all objective is to build a platform ,for making our HMR WORLD shorter and closer, created and maintained by students for the students.
